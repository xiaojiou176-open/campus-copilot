University of Washington Seattle Schedule of Classes

The Time Schedule pages contain a complete view of all credit classes at the University, and require a NetID to view. The Course Offerings pages allow a limited view of all credit classes at the University.

  • Spring Quarter 2026: Time Schedule View (NetID required) | Course Offerings View

If you know the Schedule Line Number, you can use Course Sections Status Inquiry to link directly to the detail information for that course.
